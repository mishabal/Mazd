About company
Two main Mazda plants are located in Japan (in the cities of Hiroshima and Hofu), 18 additional ones are outside. The company's production volume in 2010 amounted to 1,307,540 units, which is 32.8% higher than in 2009. Revenue for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2010 was $23.3 billion, while the company's losses for the same period were about $80 million.
